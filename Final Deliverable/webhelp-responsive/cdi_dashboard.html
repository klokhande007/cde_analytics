CDI Dashboard

Overview: A CDI Dashboard report helps the CDI Director to view the trend of various productivity and financial metrics over time and analyze the overall performance of the CDI program.

The report provides answers to the following questions.
  • Has the overall review rate improved over time?
  • Has the impact clarification rate improved over time?
  • Have the provider response and agreed rate improved over time?
  • How is my CMI trending month to month?
  • How do I pinpoint the root cause of a downward shift in CMI?
  • Is the CDI program resulting in SOI/ROM category shift?
  • What is the trend around Reimbursement Impact of the CDI program?
  • Am I performing above or below my goals?
  • How do we show the financial impact of the cases post reconciliation?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > Executive Summary > CDI Dashboard

CDI Dashboard displays the following:
  • CDI Dashboard Tabular Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the tabular chart.

For filters on all pages refer Filters on all Pages.

For View By option refer View By.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the dashboard displays the data for the inpatient encounters discharged in the last three calendar months. Click the ‘Reset’ button to reset the discharge date and select the required date.

Columns in the chart display the selected View By option.

Total column displays the row-level grand totals for the selected period. For the percentage metrics, this column displays the overall percentage for the selected period.

CDI Dashboard

The CDI Dashboard chart shows the following metrics:

Table 1. CDI Dashboard Metrics
Metric Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion criteria(if any) Tooltip
Total Encounters This metric displays the total discharges for the selected 'View By' option. Total Encounters = The total count of encounters that have a Discharge Date/time assigned.

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculation.
Encounters Reviewed This metric displays the total reviews for the selected View By option. Encounters Reviewed = The count of encounters with an initial review date/time. Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculation.
Review Rate This metric displays the percentage of total encounters reviewed for the selected View By option.

Formula: Review Rate= Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) / Total encounters (Discharges) x 100.

Note: Rounded to 0 decimal places.
Example: 89%
 Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculation.
Encounter Clarification Rate This metric displays the percentage of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED for the selected View By option.

Formula: Encounter Clarification Rate= (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED./ Count of Initial Reviews performed) *100

 Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Financial Impact Clarification Rate

This metric displays the percentage of reviewed encounters at least one financial impact clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED for the selected View By option.

Note: Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial or BOTH’ in the admin configuration is a Financial Impact Clarification.

Financial Impact Clarification Rate = (Count of reviewed encounters with at least one Financial Impact clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED / Count of encounters with an initial review date) X 100

 Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Provider Response Rate This metric displays the percentage of sent clarifications responded by the providers for each View By option.

Formula: Overall Response Rate = ((Overall Clarifications Responded/ Overall Clarifications Sent) x 100)

 Clarifications for "Review Not Needed" encounter status are not be considered for this metric calculation.

"Deleted" and "Retracted" Clarifications are not considered for this metric calculation.
Provider Agreed Rate This metric displays the percentage of responded clarifications agreed by the providers for each View By option.

Formula: Overall Agreed Rate = ((Overall Clarifications Agreed/ Overall Clarifications Responded) x 100)

 Clarifications for "Review Not Needed" encounter status are not be considered for this metric calculation.

"Deleted" and "Retracted" Clarifications are not considered for this metric calculation.
CMI Before CDI This metric displays the average of Reconciliation Assessment DRG WTs (or Working DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or Reconciliation Assessment DRG is blank) of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

CMI Before CDI = Sum of Reconciliation Assessment DRG WTs (or Working DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or Reconciliation Assessment DRG is blank) of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0). / Total count of Reconciled Encounters. (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact)

Note: This value is represented with 4 decimals.
CMI After CDI This metric displays the average of Final DRG WTs of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

CMI After CDI = Sum of Final DRG WTs of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0). / Total count of Reconciled Encounters. (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact)

Note: This value is represented with 4 decimals.
CMI Shift % This metric displays the percentage shift in weight between Final Coded DRG and Reconciliation Assessment DRG weights for Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

CMI Shift(%) = (Overall CMI After CDI- Overall CMI Before CDI /Overall Before CDI) *100

Note: Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Format: Line
Reimbursement Impact This metric displays the Reimbursement Impact of all Reconciled-Impact encounters with positive Overall CMI Shift.

Reimbursement Impact for an encounter = (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) * Blended Rate.

Reimbursement Impact = Sum of Positive Reimbursement Impact for all Reconciled Impact Encounters.

Note: Reimbursement Impact rounded to 0 decimal places.
% Cases with SOI Impact

This metric displays the percentage of reconciled cases that had an upward shift in the SOI (SOI After > SOI Before) due to CDS' work on the encounters.

Note:

Advanced SOI/ROM Values are used if Primary SOI/ROM values are blank.

% of Reconciled cases with SOI Impact= The total count of Reconciled encounters(Auto Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact and Reconciled-No Impact) for which the SOI After is greater than SOI Before./ Total count of Reconciled encounters(Auto Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact and Reconciled-No Impact)
% Cases with ROM Impact This metric displays the percentage of reconciled cases that had an upward shift in the ROM(ROM After > ROM Before) due to CDS' work on the encounters 
Note: Advanced SOI/ROM Values are used if Primary SOI/ROM values are blank.
 %  cases with ROM Impact= The total count of Reconciled encounters(Auto Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact and Reconciled-No Impact)  for which the ROM After is greater than ROM Before./ Total count of Reconciled encounters(Auto Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact and Reconciled-No Impact)
Table 2. Report Slicers
Slicer Description
Discharge Date
  • Display the discharge date range.
  • You can select date from the lookup provided.
  • Example: 7/1/2020 - 7/31/2020
  • Default: Last 3 calendar months date range selected by default.
Reset
  • With the Reset button, you can reset the discharge date range.
  • Tooltip: Use "Ctrl + Click" to reset the discharge Date.
Facility
  • Displays both active and inactive facilities in alphabetical order that you have access to.
  • You can select multiple facilities.
  • You can search the facility by Facility ID or Description.
  • Format: Facility Description (Facility ID)
  • Default: ALL
Payer
  • Displays both active and inactive payers in alphabetical order associated with the facilities you have access to.
  • You can select multiple payers.
  • "Unassigned Payer" displays data for the encounters that do not have a payer assigned.
  • You can search the Payer by Payer ID or Description.
  • Format: Payer Description (Payer ID)
  • Default: ALL

Table 5. Footnotes
Formulas Text on hover
Review Rate

Total Encounters= Count of inpatient discharges with status other than 'Review Not Needed'.

Encounters Reviewed= Count of encounters reviewed with or without clarifications.

Review Rate= (Encounters Reviewed/ Total Encounters) x 100. E.g. 89%
Encounter Clarification Rate Encounter Clarification Rate= (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED/ Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews)) x100. E.g. 33%
Financial Impact Clarification Rate Financial Impact Clarification Rate = (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one Financial Impact Clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED/Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) ) x 100). E.g. 30%
Provider Response Rate Provider Response Rate =(Count of clarifications with a Final response as Agreed, Not Agreed, Other Explanation, Alternative Specificity Diagnosis, Discuss, Sent to another Provider or Unable to Determine/Count of Clarifications Sent) x100. E.g. 90%
Provider Agreed Rate Provider Agreed Rate =(Count of clarifications with a Final response as Agreed/Count of Clarifications Responded) x100. E.g. 90%
CMI Shift

CMI Before= The avg. of R-DRG WTs (or W-DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or R-DRG is blank) of all reconciled encounters.

CMI After= The avg. of Final DRG WTs of all reconciled encounters.

CMI Shift(%)= (CMI After -CMI Before)/CMI Before X 100. E.g. 4%
Reimbursement Impact Reimbursement Impact = (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) X Blended Rate. E.g. $5,234,000
