CDS Productivity Summary

Overview: The CDS Productivity Summary Report displays the daily productivity KPIs in real time at facility level. Data in the tabular report is sorted by the CDS with the highest number of the Initial Reviews.

The report provides answers to the following business questions:
  • How is the overall performance of the CDS team in terms of review activity?
  • How is the performance of a specific CDS in terms of review activity?
  • How is the overall performance of the CDS team in terms of clarification activity?
  • How is the performance of a specific CDS in terms of clarification activity?
  • Who are my top three CDS performers in terms of the review rate

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDS General > CDS Productivity > CDS Productivity Summary.

The CDS Productivity Summary Report displays the following:
  • KPI Cards: Initial Reviews, Additional Reviews, Total Reviews, Avg Initial Review TAT (Hours)
  • Review Summary tabular chart
  • Clarification Summary tabular chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in charts.

For Global filters, refer Global Filters.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, this dashboard displays data for inpatient encounters for which activity was performed by the CDS in the last 1 week.

CDS Productivity Summary

CDS Productivity Summary - KPI Cards

Table 1. KPI Cards
KPI Card Description
Initial Reviews

The Initial Review KPI card displays count of initial reviews performed in the selected activity date range.

Initial review date/time falls within the selected activity date range.

The initial review is created when you add the working DRG (Working DRG is populated the first time) and saves the encounter in CDE application.
Additional Reviews

The Additional Reviews KPI card displays count of additional reviews performed in the selected activity date range. Additional review date/time falls within the selected activity date range.

The additional review is created when you modify the encounter by doing the following and saves it:
  • Modifying the working review
  • Creating the possible Review
  • Modifying the possible Review
  • Creating the worksheet
  • Modifying the worksheet
  • Creating a note
  • Modifying a note
  • Changing the encounter Status
  • Creating a clarification
  • Modifying a clarification
  • Creating or modifying an encounter
Total Reviews The Total Reviews KPI card displays the total reviews performed, i.e. sum of initial and additional reviews.
Average Initial Review TAT (Hours)

The Average Initial Review TAT (Hours) KPI card displays average initial review turnaround time in hours.

Initial review TAT (Hours) for an encounter = Initial review date/time of the encounter – Admit date/time of the encounter in hours.

Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters = Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters reviewed within the selected activity date range.

Average initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters = Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters / Total number of initial reviews performed within the selected activity date range.
CDS Productivity Summary - Review Summary tabular chart
Table 2. Review Summary Table Column and Description
Column Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria (if any) Tooltip
CDS Displays the name of the activity CDS who performed at least one review (initial or additional) in the activity date range. N/A
Encounters Displays the count of encounters reviewed by the CDS in the activity date range. In other words, count of encounters for which the CDS did any activity (initial review or additional review) in the activity date range.
Initial Reviews

Displays the count of Initial Reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range.

Note: Count of Initial Reviews performed in the activity date range cannot be more than the count of Encounters Reviewed in that date range.
Additional Reviews Displays the count of additional Reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range.
Total Reviews Displays the sum of initial and additional reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range.
Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hours)

Displays the average Initial Review TAT (Hours) for the CDS.

Initial Review TAT (Hours) for the encounter = (Admit Date/Time – Initial Review Date/Time) in hours

Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hours) for the CDS = Sum of ‘Initial Review TAT (Hours)’ of all the initial reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range / Total Initial reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range.
CDS Productivity Summary - Clarification Summary tabular chart
Table 3. Clarification Summary Table Column and Description
Column Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria (if any) Tooltip
CDS This column displays the name of the initiating CDS who sent at least one clarification in the activity date range. N/A
Clarification Sent

For each CDS, this column displays the count of clarifications with at least one instance sent in the activity date range.

Note: Instance Sent date falls in the activity date range.
Encounters with Clarification

This column displays the count of reviewed encounters for which the CDS sent at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED in the activity date range.

 The count of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED.
Encounters with Impact Clarifications This column displays the count of encounters for which the CDS sent at least one instance for the Financial Impact Clarification in the activity date range.
Note: Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial or BOTH’ in the admin configuration is an Impact Clarification.
 Encounters with Impact Clarification = Count of reviewed encounters with at least one financial impact clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED.
Impact Clarification Rate

Out of total encounters reviewed by the CDS in the selected activity date range, the percentage of Encounters with Impact Clarifications.

Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial or BOTH’ in the admin configuration is an Impact Clarification.

 Impact Clarification Rate = (Count of encounters for which the CDS sent at least one instance for the Financial Impact Clarification in the activity date range/ The count of Initial Reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range) X 100
Financial Impact Clarifications Sent For each CDS, this column displays the count of Financial Impact clarifications with at least one instance sent in the activity date range.
Note: Instance Sent date falls in the activity date range. Financial Impact Clarifications are the clarifications with a standard or custom type (Primary Type at Reconciliation) set-up as 'Financial' in the admin configuration.
Impact Clarification Agreed Rate

Out of total Impact Clarifications Responded by the CDS in the selected activity date range, the percentage of Impact Clarifications Agreed.

Note: Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial’ in the admin configuration is an Impact Clarification.

 Impact Clarification Agreed Rate = (Out of Impact Clarifications Responded in the activity date range, count of impact clarifications with at least one agreed instance / Out of the Financial Impact Clarifications Sent by the CDS in the activity date range, count of impact clarifications with at least one responded instance (response other than Pending, Retracted, and No Response) in the activity date range) x 100
SEV Clarification Rate Out of total encounters reviewed by the CDS in the selected activity date range, the percentage of Encounters with SEV Clarifications.
Note: SEV Clarifications are the clarifications that have Primary Type at Reconciliation as SEV.
 SEV Clarification Rate = (Count of encounters for which the CDS sent at least one instance for the SEV Clarification in the activity date range/ The count of Initial Reviews performed by the CDS in the activity date range) X 100
