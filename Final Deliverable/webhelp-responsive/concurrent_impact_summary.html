Concurrent Impact

Overview: Concurrent Impact report helps a CDS Manager to analyze the financial impact of the CDI program. It provides insights of the impact the CDS's are having concurrently with the patient stay.

The report provides answers to the following questions:
  • What is the financial impact the CDI team is having concurrently with the patient stay?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > CDI Operations > Concurrent Impact.

Concurrent Impact displays the following:
  • Concurrent Impact Summary Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the table.

For filters all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the dashboard displays the data for the inpatient encounters admitted in the last one calendar month. Click the ‘Reset’ button to reset the admit date and select the required date.

Concurrent Impact Summary

Concurrent Impact Summary

  • This tabular chart displays the concurrent impact summary for each Facility and Payer.
  • Data organization:
    • By default, the chart displays the data for Facility and Payer (indented under Facility names).
    • Click the ‘Drill Up’ arrow to view the Facility level summary.
    • Click the ‘Go to the next level in the hierarchy’ arrow to view the Payer level summary.
    • Click the ‘Expand all down one level in the hierarchy’ to view the summary by Facility and Payer.
  • Row totals: Displays the overall calculations for each column.
  • Sort: Data is sorted based on Facility Description in ascending order. Within each facility, data is sorted based on Payer Description in ascending order.
  • Info icon(text on hover): Encounter Status: Discharged, In Progress, Review Complete, Ready to Reconcile and Reconciliation-Hold status.

Concurrent Encounters

The total count of encounters with an Initial Review Date/Time and status as Discharged, In Progress, Review Complete, Ready to Reconcile and Reconciliation-Hold status.

Note: Encounters that do not have an initial review date ( 'Working DRG' is not assigned OR Working DRG = Null/Blank) are not counted.

Total Count of Concurrent Encounters = The count of encounters with Initial Review Date/Time and status as Discharged, In Progress, Review Complete, Ready to Reconcile and Reconciliation-Hold status.
Encounters with Financial Impact Clarifications

The count of reviewed encounters with at least one Financial Impact clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED.

Note: Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial or BOTH’ in the admin configuration is a Financial Impact Clarification.

Clarifications with Status 'DELETED' or 'RETRACTED' are excluded from the count.

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Financial Impact Clarifications Sent

The count of Financial Impact clarifications with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED sent for the reviewed encounters.

Note: Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial’ in the admin configuration is a Financial Impact Clarification.

Clarifications with Status 'DELETED' or 'RETRACTED' are excluded from the count.

Clarifications for "Review Not Needed" encounter status are not considered for this metric calculation.
Total Potential Reimbursement

The sum of Potential Reimbursement values of all concurrent cases for the facility and payer for the selected time period.

The total potential reimbursement resulting from shift in the weight between Working DRG and Possible DRG weights for Encounters that have an initial review date and status of In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

If Possible DRG Weight is blank, Working DRG Weight is considered.

Note: Overall Reimbursement Impact ($) can be either positive or negative.

Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Total Concurrent Reimbursement Impact ($) = Concurrent CMI Shift * Blended Rate
Total Potential Reimbursement at 75%

The total potential reimbursement at 75% resulting from shift in the weight between Working DRG and Possible DRG weights for Encounters that have an initial review date and status of In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Note: Negative ‘Total Potential Reimbursement($)’ is displayed in the red font.

Total potential Reimbursement Impact ($) at 75% = (Total Concurrent Reimbursement Impact($) )*0.75
Potential CMI Before CDI

The average of Working DRG weights of all encounters that have an initial review date and Status of In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Rounded to 4 decimal places.

Example: 0.7112

Potential CMI Before CDI = (Sum of Working DRG WTs of all encounters that have an initial review date and are in the status of Status: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold. / Count of encounters that have an initial review date and are in the status of In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.)

Encounters with status In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold for which Working DRG is blank are not considered for this metric calculation.
Potential CMI After CDI

The average of Possible DRG weights of all encounters that have an initial review date and Status of: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Rounded to 4 decimal places. Example: 0.7112

Potential CMI After CDI = (Sum of Possible DRG WTs [or Working DRG WTs when Possible DRG is blank] of all encounters that have an initial review date and are in the status of Status: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold. / Count of encounters that have an initial review date and of Status: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.)

Encounters with status In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold for which Working DRG is blank are not considered for this metric calculation.
Potential CMI Shift

The shift in weight between Possible DRG and Working DRG weights for Encounters that have an initial review date and Status of: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Note: This value is rounded to 4 decimals places.

Potential CMI Shift= (Concurrent CMI After CDI- Concurrent CMI Before CDI)
Potential CMI Shift (%)

The percentage shift in weight between Possible DRG and Working DRG weights for Encounters that have an initial review date and Status of: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Note: Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Negative ‘Potential CMI Shift (%)’ is displayed in red font.

Potential CMI Shift(%) = (Concurrent CMI After CDI- Concurrent CMI Before CDI /Concurrent CMI Before CDI) *100
Potential CMI Shift (%) at 75%

The percentage shift at 75% in weight between Possible DRG and Working DRG weights for Encounters that have an initial review date and Status of: In Progress, Discharged, Ready to Reconcile, Review Complete and Reconciliation Hold.

Note: Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Negative ‘Potential CMI Shift at 75%’ is displayed in the red font.

CMI Shift (%) at 75% = (Concurrent CMI After CDI- Concurrent CMI Before CDI /Concurrent CMI Before CDI) *0.75
Table 2. Report Slicers
Slicer Description
Admit Date
  • Displays the admit date range here. You can select required dates from the date lookup.
  • Example: 7/1/2020 - 7/31/2020
  • Default: Last 1 calendar months date range selected by default.
Reset
  • With the Reset button, you can reset the admit date range.
  • Tooltip: Use "Ctrl + Click" to reset the Admit Date.
Facility
  • Displays a list of active and inactive facilities that you have access to. The list is sorted alphabetically.
  • You can select multiple facilities
  • You can search the facility by Facility ID or Description.
  • Format: Facility Description (Facility ID)
  • Default: ALL
Payer
  • Displays a list of active and inactive Payers associated with the facilities that you have access to. The list is sorted alphabetically.
  • You can select multiple payers
  • "Unassigned Payer" displays data for the encounters that do not have a payer assigned.
  • You can search the Payer by Payer ID or Description.
  • Format: Payer Description (Payer ID)
  • Default: ALL
CDS
  • Displays a list of active and inactive CDSs associated with the facilities you have access to.
  • The CDS Filter in all the reports displays the users with at-least CDS or CDS Manager role assigned.
  • "Unassigned CDS" displays data for the encounters that do not have a CDS assigned.
  • Format: Last Name, First Name MI (CDS ID)
  • Default: ALL
Potential Reimbursement Impact
  • Displays a list of Potential Reimbursement Impact. You can select a value from the following:
    • Positive Impact
    • Negative Impact
    • No Impact
  • Upon selection of the ‘Positive Impact’ option from the filter, the report displays the data for the Reconciled-Impact encounters with positive Potential Reimbursement Impact($).
  • Upon selection of the ‘Negative Impact’ option from the filter, the report displays the data for the Reconciled-Impact encounters with negative Potential Reimbursement Impact($).
  • Upon selection of the ‘No Impact’ option from the filter, the report displays the data for the Reconciled-Impact encounters in which the Potential Reimbursement Impact($) is zero.
  • Default: ALL
