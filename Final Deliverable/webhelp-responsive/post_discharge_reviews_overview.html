Post-Discharge Reviews Overview

Overview: A Post-Discharge Review report helps a CDI manager to analyze the statistics around encounters that presents an opportunity to enhance reimbursement prior to final billing and post-discharge review activity.

The report provides answers to the following questions.
  • How many encounters were presented for review before final billing?
  • How many post-discharge reviews were performed?
  • What percentage of presented cases were reviewed by the reviewer?
  • What percentage of presented cases were for a specific opportunity? What percentage of presented cases were for PDX sequencing opportunities?
  • What percentage of presented cases were resolved by identifying reimbursable secondary diagnosis?
  • What are the details around the encounters in which the DRG/WT arrived at by the Coder (Final coded) is different from the final billed DRG, DRG WT?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE Triage > Post-Discharge Reviews > Post-Discharge Reviews Overview.

Post-Discharge Reviews Overview report displays the following:
  • KPI Cards: Encounters with Impact Opportunity, Encounters Reviewed, Encounters Impacted, Overall Reimbursement Impact
  • Impact Opportunities Identified and Addressed Chart
  • Post-Discharge Review Details Tabular Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the charts.

For filters on all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Post-Discharge Reviews Overview

Post-Discharge Reviews Overview - KPI Cards
Table 1. KPI Cards
KPI Card Description
Encounters with Impact Opportunity

The count of encounters with at-least one post-discharge review opportunity identified.

This KPI displays the count of encounters presented to the Post-Discharge Review worklist.
Encounters Reviewed

The count and percentage of encounters reviewed by the post-discharge case reviewer.

  • Format: Count of Encounters Reviewed (Percentage of Encounters with Impact Opportunity reviewed)
  • Count: Out of total 'Encounters with Impact Opportunity', the count of encounters with at-least one resolved opportunity or 'No Further Action Required' checked.
  • Percentage: (Encounters Reviewed/ Encounters with Impact Opportunity) x 100) = (Count of encounters with at-least one resolved opportunity / Count of encounters with at-least one post-discharge review opportunity identified) x 100) Rounded to 0 decimal places. E.g. 10%
Encounters Impacted

The count and percentage of reviewed encounters for which the post-discharge review resulted in a higher reimbursement.

Out of Encounters Reviewed, the count of encounters in which the Final Billed DRG WT was greater than the Final Coded DRG WT.

  • Format: Count of Encounters Impacted (Percentage of Encounters with Impact Opportunity impacted)
  • Count: Out of total 'Encounters with Impact Opportunity', the count of encounters with Final Billed DRG WT greater than the Final Coded DRG WT.
  • Percentage: (Encounters Impacted / Encounters with Impact Opportunity) x 100) = ( Count of encounters with Final Billed DRG WT greater than the Final Coded DRG WT / Count of encounters with at-least one post-discharge review opportunity identified) x 100) Rounded to 0 decimal places. Example: 10%
Overall Reimbursement Impact

The total expected reimbursement resulting from shift in the weight between Final Billed DRG and Final Coded DRG weights for Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) in which Final Billed WT >= Final Coded WT (Final Billed WT - Final Coded WT >= 0)

Overall Reimbursement Impact Post-Discharge = (The sum of Reimbursement Impact ($) of all Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) with positive Overall WT Shift) * Blended Rate

Note: Total Reimbursement Impact ($) rounded to 0 decimal places.

Encounters with negative WT shift i.e. (Final Billed WT - Final Coded WT < 0) are not considered in this metric calculation.

Example: $ 5,234.00

Post-Discharge Reviews Overview - Impact Opportunities Identified and Addressed Chart

  • The ‘Impact Opportunities Identified and Addressed ’ chart displays the count of opportunities identified and addressed by the reviewer for each impact opportunity type.
  • X-axis: Displays the description of the Opportunities Identified Example: Resequencing PDx
  • Y-axis: Displays the count of opportunities.
  • Sort: Chart is sorted by the opportunity type with the highest identified opportunities in descending order.

Post-Discharge Reviews Overview - Post-Discharge Review Details Tabular Chart

This chart displays the details of the encounters presented to the Post-Discharge review worklist (i.e. list of encounters with at-least one post-discharge review opportunity identified).

  • Format: Tabular
  • One row in the table corresponds to one encounter.

Column Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria (if any) Tooltip
Facility The facility assigned to the encounter.

Account No. The Account Number assigned to the encounter.
MRN The MRN assigned to the encounter.

No Further Action Required

‘Y’ gets displayed when the reviewer has selected ‘No Further Action Required’ check box on the Post-Discharge Review Checklist screen.

Note: Not all the reviewed cases have 'No Further Action Required' checked.
R-DRG

The code and description of the Reconciliation Assessment DRG displayed on the Reconciliation page.

Example: 872 - SEPTICEMIA OR SEVERE SEPSIS WITHOUT MV >96 HOURS WITHOUT MAJOR COMPLICATION OR COMORBIDITY (MCC)
R-DRG WT

The WT associated with the Reconciliation Assessment DRG displayed on the Reconciliation page.

Example: 1.0393
Final Coded DRG

The code and description of the Final Coded DRG displayed on the Reconciliation page.

Example: 872 - SEPTICEMIA OR SEVERE SEPSIS WITHOUT MV >96 HOURS WITHOUT MAJOR COMPLICATION OR COMORBIDITY (MCC)
Final Coded DRG WT

The WT associated with the Final Coded DRG displayed on the Reconciliation page.

Example: 1.0393
Final Billed DRG

The code and description of the Final Billed DRG.

Example: 872 - SEPTICEMIA OR SEVERE SEPSIS WITHOUT MV >96 HOURS WITHOUT MAJOR COMPLICATION OR COMORBIDITY (MCC)
Final Billed DRG WT

The WT associated with the Final Billed DRG.

Example: 1.0393

Final Billed DRG WT > Final Coded DRG WT (Final Billed DRG WT is displayed in green font.)

Final Billed DRG WT < Final Coded DRG WT (Final Billed DRG WT is displayed in orange font.)

Final Billed DRG WT = Final Coded DRG WT (Final Billed DRG WT is displayed in normal font.)
Variance Shift in the weight between Final Billed DRG WT and Final Coded DRG WT.
  • Variance = Final Billed DRG WT - Final Coded DRG WT
  • Rounded to 4 decimal places.

    Example: 1.0178

  • Negative ‘Variance’ is displayed in the red font.
Blended Rate

The Blended Rate associated with the payer/Grouper assigned to the encounter.

When the Blended Rate is not available, the Operating Federal rate is displayed.
Post-Discharge Review Reimbursement Impact

The ‘Post-Discharge Review Reimbursement Impact ’ value for the encounter.

'Reimbursement Impact' for an encounter is calculated using the below formula:

  • Reimbursement Impact = Variance x Blended Rate
  • Rounded to 0 decimal places.

    Example: $ 5,234

  • Negative ‘Reimbursement Impact’ is displayed in the red font.

    Example: $ 5,234
Encounter Status

The Encounter Status assigned to the encounter.

Example: Reconciled No Impact
Payer

The Payer assigned to the encounter.

Example: Medicare (MED)
Unit

The Unit assigned to the encounter.

Example: PODA
