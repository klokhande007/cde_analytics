CMI Breakdown

Overview: CMI Breakdown dashboard allows CDI Directors and Managers to analyze the trend of Medical CMI and Surgical CMI over time.

The report provides answers to the following questions.
  • What is the breakdown of medical vs. surgical cases?
  • What % of reconciled cases were medical?
  • What is the breakdown of medical vs. surgical CMI?
  • How is my Medical CMI trending month-to-month?
  • How is my Surgical CMI trending month-to-month?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > Executive Summary > CMI Breakdown.

CMI Breakdown dashboard displays the following:

  • KPI Cards: Overall CMI Before CDI, Overall CMI After CDI, CMI Shift, CMI Shift (%)
  • Medical and Surgical Cases Chart
  • CMI Shift Chart
  • CMI Trend Tabular Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the charts and table.

For filters on all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Overall CMI Before CDI

The average of Reconciliation Assessment DRG WTs (or Working DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or Reconciliation Assessment DRG is blank) of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

(Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact)
Overall CMI After CDI

The average of Final DRG WTs of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

Note: This value is represented with 4 decimals.

Overall CMI After CDI = Sum of Final DRG WTs of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0). / Total count of Reconciled Encounters. (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact)
CMI Shift

Shift in weight between Final Coded DRG and Reconciliation Assessment DRG weights for Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

Note: This value is represented with 4 decimals.

Overall CMI Shift= (Overall CMI After CDI- Overall CMI Before CDI)
CMI Shift (%)

Percentage shift in weight between Final Coded DRG and Reconciliation Assessment DRG weights for Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

Note: Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Format: 4%, 3% etc.

Overall CMI Shift(%) = (Overall CMI After CDI- Overall CMI Before CDI /Overall Before CDI) *100

CMI Breakdown - Medical and Surgical Cases Chart

The ‘Medical and Surgical Cases’ chart displays the volume and percentage of Medical and Surgical cases for each month.

  • X-axis: Displays the discharge month in ascending order. (Format: Jun 2020, Jul 2020, etc.)
  • Y-axis: Displays the percentage of encounters.

  • By default, the chart displays the data for 3 calendar months.

  • Info Icon(text on hover): Medical Cases: Encounters with Final Coded DRG as Medical. Surgical Cases: Encounters with Final Coded DRG as Surgical. Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled.
Note: Medical encounters are those for which Final Coded DRG is Medical (M) ( if M/S indicator is not available for Final DRG and Final DRG Code = Assessment DRG Code, then Assessment DRG is Medical. if M/S indicator is not available for Final DRG and Assessment DRG, and Final DRG Code = Working DRG Code, then Working DRG is Medical)
Note: Surgical encounters are those for which Final Coded DRG is Surgical (S) ( if M/S indicator is not available for Final DRG and Final DRG Code = Assessment DRG Code, then Assessment DRG is Medical. ( if M/S indicator is not available for Final DRG and Final DRG Code = Assessment DRG Code, then Assessment DRG is Surgical. OR if M/S indicator is not available for Final DRG and Assessment DRG, and Final DRG Code = Working DRG Code, then Working DRG is Surgical)

  • Displays both active and inactive facilities in alphabetical order that you have access to.

  • Displays both active and inactive payers in alphabetical order associated with the facilities you have access to.

  • "Unassigned Payer" displays data for the encounters that do not have a payer assigned.
  • You can search the Payer by Payer ID or Description.
  • Format: Payer Description (Payer ID)
  • Default: ALL

Overall CMI Before/After

CMI Before CDI= The avg. of R-DRG WTs (or W-DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or R-DRG is blank) of all reconciled encounters. E.g. 1.2717
