Missed Concurrent Reviews

Overview: The Missed Concurrent Reviews report identifies encounters that are discharged prior to review and therefore missed an opportunity for documentation improvement.

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE Triage > CDE Triage Dashboard > Missed Concurrent Reviews

This Missed Concurrent Reviews report displays the following:
  • Summary Cards: Discharges, Missed Reviews, %Missed Reviews
  • Missed Concurrent Reviews by CDS Chart
  • Missed Concurrent Reviews by Rank at Discharge Chart
  • Encounter details Tabular Chart

For filters on all pages, refer Page Level filters.

Missed Concurrent Reviews - Missed Concurrent Reviews by CDS

  • In the Missed Concurrent Reviews by CDS section, X-axis indicates the count of the encounters, and Y-axis indicates the name of the CDS assigned to the encounter in CDS Triage.
  • Encounters that do not have any CDS assigned are displayed under the Unassigned CDS category on the Y-axis.

Missed Concurrent Reviews - Missed Concurrent Reviews by Rank at Discharge

  • In the Missed Concurrent Reviews by Rank at Discharge section, the donut chart displays the count of missed reviews for each rank at discharge. Tooltip displays the count of missed reviews for that rank at discharge.
  • The Summary Card at the top of the Missed Concurrent Reviews report displays the total number of discharged encounters, total missed reviews, and the % of the missed reviews between the selected date range.
  • Formula: Missed reviews (%) = (Total missed reviews/ Total discharges) × 100
