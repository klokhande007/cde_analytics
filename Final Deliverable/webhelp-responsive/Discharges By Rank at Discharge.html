Discharges By Rank at Discharge

Overview: The Discharges By Rank At Discharge report helps to identify the count of discharged encounters for each Rank at Discharge.

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE Triage > CDE Triage Dashboard > Discharges By Rank at Discharge.

Discharges By Rank At Discharge displays the following:
  • Summary Card
  • Discharges By Rank at Discharge Chart

For filters on all pages, refer Page Level filters.

Default Display:

Discharges By Rank at Discharge

  • X-axis indicates the Rank at Discharge, and Y-axis indicates the count of encounter in each financial class.
  • Encounters that do not have any Rank at Discharge assigned are displayed under No Rank category on the X-axis.

Discharges By Rank at Discharge - Summary Card

  • The Summary Card at the right side of the Discharges By Rank At Discharge report displays the total number of discharged encounters, total reviews, and the review rate between the selected date range.
  • Formula: Review rate = (Total Reviews/ Total Discharges) × 100.
