Reviews by Payer

Overview: The Reviews by Payer report helps to identify payers and financial class with highest number of priority reviews. This report displays the count of encounters reviewed by the CDSs for each payer and rank.

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE Triage > CDE Triage Dashboard > Reviews by Payer.

The Reviews by Payer displays the following:
  • Reviews by Payer Chart
  • Reviews by Payer by Highest Rank Chart
  • % Reviews by Financial Class Chart
  • Encounter details Tabular Chart

For filters on all pages, refer Page Level filters.

Default Display:

Reviews by Payer

Reviews by Payer - Reviews by Payer Chart

  • In the Reviews by Payer section, the donut chart displays the percentage of reviewed encounters for each payer.
  • Tooltip displays count of reviews for each payer.

Reviews by Payer - Reviews by Payer by Highest Rank Chart

  • In the Reviews by Payer by Highest Rank section, X-axis indicates the name of the Payer assigned to the encounter in CDS Triage and Y-axis indicates the percent of encounters reviewed by the CDS for each payer and rank.
  • If the description of the payer is not available, system displays the payer ID.
  • Encounters that do not have any payer assigned are displayed under the Unassigned Payer category on the Y-axis.

Reviews by Payer - % Reviews by Financial Class

  • In the % Reviews by Financial Class section, X-axis indicates the percentage of reviewed encounters and Y-axis indicates the name of the financial class assigned to the encounter.
  • Encounters that do not have any financial class assigned are displayed under the Unassigned Financial Class category on the Y-axis.
