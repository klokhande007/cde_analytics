Clarification Details

Overview: Clarification Details report enables CDI Manager to analyze the details around the clarifications, identify gaps in the clarification process, and identify educational opportunities for the CDSs.

The report provides answers to the following questions.
  • What are the details around the clarifications created by the CDSs?
  • How do I get a list of clarifications that do not have a final response?
  • How do I get a list of clarifications that were responded post-discharge?
  • How do I get a list of all the impact clarifications sent by the CDSs?
  • How do I get a list of all the clarifications that were not responded to by the providers?
  • How do we find the gaps in the clarification process?
  • Which CDSs are not following the clarification best practices?
  • Which CDS could benefit from additional education on the clarification process?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > CDI Operations > Clarification Details.

Clarification Details displays the following:
  • KPI Cards: Clarifications Sent, Not Final
  • Encounter and Clarification Instance Details

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the table.

For filters all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes details, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the dashboard displays the data for the inpatient encounters admitted in the last one calendar month. Click the ‘Reset’ button to reset both admit date and discharge date and select the required date. Select slicers on the top to further analyze the data.
