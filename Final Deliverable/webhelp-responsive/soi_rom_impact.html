SOI/ROM Impact

Overview: SOI/ROM Impact dashboard enables CDI leaders to assess the program impact on severity and risk of mortality. A SOI/ROM Impact dashboard helps the CDI Director to view the SOI/ROM trends that resulted from the CDS review activity and analyze the overall performance of the CDI program.

The report provides answers to the following questions.

  • Is the CDI program resulting in an appropriate shift to higher SOI/ROM categories?
  • How is my SOI/ROM before and after CDI trending?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > Executive Summary > SOI/ROM Impact.

SOI/ROM Impact displays the following:
  • KPI Cards: % Cases with SOI Impact, % Cases with ROM Impact
  • SOI Impact Chart
  • ROM Impact Chart
  • SOI Before CDI Trend Chart
  • ROM Before CDI Trend Chart
  • SOI After CDI Trend Chart
  • ROM After CDI Trend Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the tabular chart.

For filters on all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For information about footnotes, refer Footnotes details.

SOI/ROM Impact - SOI Impact Chart

The SOI Impact charts displays the percentage of reconciled encounters (Auto Reconciled, Reconciled Impact, Reconciled- No Impact) with SOI Before CDI and percentage of reconciled encounters with SOI after CDI for each SOI Category for the selected date range.
  • X-axis: Displays the values in the format: SOI 3, SOI 4 etc.
  • Y-axis: Displays the percentage of reconciled encounters.
  • Sort Order: Data in the chart is sorted by SOI value in ascending order.
  • Info icon(text on hover): Percentage of reconciled cases that had an upward shift in the SOI (SOI After > SOI Before) due to CDS' work on the encounters. Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled.
