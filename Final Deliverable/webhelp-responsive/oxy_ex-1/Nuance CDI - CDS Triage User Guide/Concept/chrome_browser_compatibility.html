Chrome Browser Settings

Nuance CDE One is also compatible with the Google Chrome browser. To Download Chrome refer Installing Google Chrome and follow the steps as per section First Time Sign In for logging in.

CDE One minimum supported Version for Google Chrome

Version 83.0.4103.97 (Official Build) (64-bit)

How to know your current version for Google Chrome

Click the three-dot icon on the top-right corner and click Help > About Google Chrome.

How to update Google Chrome to latest version

  1. On your computer, open Google Chrome.
  2. At the top-right corner, click .
  3. Click Update Google Chrome.
    Note: If you can't find this option, you're on the latest version.
  4. Click Relaunch.
    Note: The browser saves your opened tabs and windows and reopens them automatically when it restarts. Your incognito windows won't reopen when Chrome restarts. If you'd prefer not to restart straight away, click Not now. The next time you restart your browser, the update gets applied.

How to allow Pop-ups

  1. On your computer, open Chrome.
  2. At the top right, click and then click Settings.
  3. Under Privacy and security, click Site Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Pop-ups and redirects.
  5. At the top, turn the setting to Allowed.
  6. Click Add to Allow some websites as trusted sites.
  7. You can add the following list of URLs as trusted sites.
    • https://<customer>.nuancecdi.com - Used by your CDE One production environment
    • https://staging-<customer>.nuancecdi.com - Used by your CDE One staging environment
    • https://nuancehdp.auth0.com - Auth0 endpoint used for Single Sign-On
    • https://dc.services.visualstudio.com - Used by Microsoft Application Insights analytics
    • https://app.powerbi.com - Used by Microsoft Power BI Analytics
    • https://az416426.vo.msecnd.net - Used by Microsoft Power BI Analytics
    • https://*.analysis.windows.net - Used by Microsoft Power BI Analytics
    • https://content.powerapps.com - Used for Microsoft Power BI Analytics
    • https://visuals.azureedge.net - Used for Microsoft Power BI Analytics
    • https://d1vy0qa05cdjr5.cloudfront.net - CDE online training
    • https://kb-shared.nuancecdi.com
    • *.salesforceliveagent.com- Salesforce support
    • *.force.com- Salesforce support
    • nuance.salesforceliveagent.com - Salesforce Live Chat

How to Print Page with Chrome

  1. On your computer, open Chrome.
  2. At the top right, click and then click Print.
  3. Make changes to the settings on the right side panel and click Print.
