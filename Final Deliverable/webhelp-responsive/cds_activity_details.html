CDS Activity Details

Overview: CDS Activity Details Report displays the CDS review activity details, how often a CDS reviewed the encounter and TAT.

This reports answers the following business questions:
  • How soon after admission the initial review was performed?
  • What is the average TAT of initial reviews?
  • Are there cases that are never getting reviewed? (untouched)
  • What is the detail around the reviews?
  • How many total reviews were performed?
  • Were the reviews were concurrent or retrospective?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDS General > CDS Productivity > CDS Activity Details.

The CDS Activity Details Report displays the following:
  • KPI cards: Initial Reviews, Additional Reviews, Total Reviews, Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hours)
  • CDS Activity Details Tabular Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report slicers) and the corresponding data gets filtered in charts.

For filters all pages, refer Global Filters.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, this dashboard displays data for inpatient encounters for which activity was performed by the CDS in the last 1 week.

CDS Activity Details

CDS Activity Details - KPI Cards

Table 1. KPI Cards
KPI Card Description
Initial Reviews

The Initial Review KPI card displays count of initial reviews performed in the selected activity date range.

Initial review date/time falls within the selected activity date range.

The initial review is created when you add the working DRG (Working DRG is populated the first time) and saves the encounter in CDE application.
Additional Reviews

The Additional Reviews KPI card displays count of additional reviews performed in the selected activity date range. Additional review date/time falls within the selected activity date range.

The additional review is created when you modify the encounter by doing the following and saves it:
  • Modifying the working review
  • Creating the possible Review
  • Modifying the possible Review
  • Creating the worksheet
  • Modifying the worksheet
  • Creating a note
  • Modifying a note
  • Changing the encounter Status
  • Creating a clarification
  • Modifying a clarification
  • Creating or modifying an encounter
Total Reviews The Total Reviews KPI card displays the total reviews performed, i.e. sum of initial and additional reviews.
Average Initial Review TAT (Hours)

The Average Initial Review TAT (Hours) KPI card displays average initial review turnaround time in hours.

Initial review TAT (Hours) for an encounter = Initial review date/time of the encounter – Admit date/time of the encounter in hours.

Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters = Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters reviewed within the selected activity date range.

Average initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters = Total initial review TAT (Hours) for all the encounters / Total number of initial reviews performed within the selected activity date range.
