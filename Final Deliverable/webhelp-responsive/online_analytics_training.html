Product Training

These courses are useful for CDI Managers and CDSs who will be evaluating CDE Analytics for their program management. New courses will be added here to cover more in-depth topics as they are developed.

  • CDE Analytics: Reporting Basics

    This course gives you an introduction to CDE Analytics. It contains the tools you need to access CDE Analytics and start digging into the reports, covering how to access CDE Analytics, an overview of your daily and weekly monitoring "go-to" reports, and insight into what drives the reporting data. This course also includes a navigation video highlighting the different data visualizations, plus basic filtering, default data and export options.
