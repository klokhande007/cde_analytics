CDE Triage Reports Overview

  • CDE Triage Reports answers the following questions:
    Triage Utilization
    • Are the CDSs performing timely reviews of highest priority encounters?
    • For which priority Ranks most reviews were done?
    • When encounters are discharged what is the most frequent rank assigned at Discharge?
    • Out of total encounters reviewed for a payer, what percentage was Rank 1 and Rank 2 and so on?
    Assistant Adoption
    • How well accepted CLU suggested codes are within CDS Triage?
    • Who are the CDSs who are using CDS Assistant the least?
    • Who are the CDSs who are using CDS Assistant the most, by accepting and/or rejecting the most CLU suggestions?
    • What were the top rejected codes?
    • Who are the CDSs rejecting the most rejected codes?
    • Who are the non-utilizing CDSs, who haven't accepted and/or rejected and or Hidden any CLU

    CDE Triage Reports answers the following business questions:

    • How well the CLU suggested codes are accepted within CDS Triage?
    • The CDSs using CDS Assistant the least.
    • The CDSs using CDS Assistant the most, by accepting and/or rejecting the most CLU suggestions.
    • The top rejected codes.
    • The CDSs rejecting the most rejected codes.
    • The non-utilizing CDSs, who haven't accepted and/or rejected and/or Hidden any CLU suggestion.
    • If the CDSs are performing timely reviews of highest priority encounters?
    • Ranks where most reviews were done.
    • The most frequent rank assigned at Discharge.
    • Calculate the percentage of each rank out of total encounters reviewed for a payer.

Page Level filters are used to customize the CDS Triage dashboard and reports. Filters can be applied on the dashboard as well as to the individual reports.

Note:
  • CDE Triage reports are applicable only for inpatient encounters.
  • Following types of encounters are excluded from the CDE Triage reports: Review Not Needed, Unassigned (Encounter status is Untouched)
Table 1. Page Level filters
Filter Description
Admission Date Displays admit date of the encounter.
Attending Provider Displays providers in alphabetical order associated with the facilities which are assigned to you. Unassigned Provider display encounters that do not have a Attending Provider assigned.
CDS Displays both active and inactive CDSs in alphabetical order associated with the facilities which are assigned to you. CDS name is displayed in following format: Last Name, First Name MI (CDS user ID). Unassigned CDS display encounters that do not have a CDS assigned.
Discharge Date Displays discharge date of the encounter.
Encounter Status Displays the current status of the encounter. Following encounter status are displayed:
  • Reconciled Impact
  • Reconciled No Impact
  • Auto Reconciled
  • In Progress
  • Discharged
  • Follow up
  • Provider Clarification
Facility Displays facilities which are assigned to you.
Financial Class Displays financial class associated with the encounter. Unassigned Financial Class display encounters that do not have a Financial Class assigned.
Payer Displays both active and inactive payers in alphabetical order associated with the facilities which are assigned to you. Unassigned Payer display encounters that do not have a payer assigned.
Suggestion Type Displays all the available suggestion types (CC, MCC, HAC etc). This filter is applicable only for Acceptance Rate report.
Suggestion_Ind

Displays 1 or 0.

1: Data for only the codes in the 'Suggested' and 'Quality' categories is displayed.

0: Data for all categories are displayed. (Suggested, Quality, Other).

Default: 1

This filter is applicable only for Acceptance Usage report.
