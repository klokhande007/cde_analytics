Executive Overview

Overview: An Executive Overview dashboard assists a CDI Director in analyzing the overall performance of the CDI program. The dashboard also helps you group data by week, month, quarter, and year and analyze trends over different time periods and analyze them more easily.

The report provides answers to the following questions.
  • Has the overall review rate improved over time?
  • Has the impact clarification rate improved over time?
  • How is my CMI trending month to month?
  • How do I pinpoint the root cause of a downward shift in CMI?
  • Is the CDI program resulting in SOI/ROM category shift?
  • What is the trend around Reimbursement Impact of the CDI program?
  • Am I performing above or below my goals?
  • How do we show the financial impact of the cases post reconciliation?

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > Executive Summary > Executive Overview

Executive Overview displays the following:.
  • KPI Cards: Overall CMI Shift, Overall Reimb Impact, Overall Review Rate, Overall -Clarification Rate
  • Review Rate Chart
  • Clarification Rate Chart
  • Provider Response and Agreed Rate Chart
  • CMI Shift Chart
  • Reimbursement Impact ($) Chart
  • SOI/ROM Impact Chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Slicers on the Report) and the corresponding data gets generated in the tabular chart.

For View By option, refer View By.

For filters on all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the dashboard displays the data for the inpatient encounters discharged in the last three calendar months. Click the Reset button to reset the discharge date and select the required date.

Executive Overview - KPI Cards

Table 1. KPI Card details
KPI Card Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria(if any)
Overall CMI Shift This metric, by default displays the Overall CMI Shift for inpatient encounters with discharges in the last 9 calendar months.

Formula: Overall CMI Shift(%) = (Overall CMI After CDI- Overall CMI Before CDI /Overall Before CDI) *100

Note:
Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Format: 4%, 3% and so on.
Overall Reimb Impact

This metric displays Overall Reimbursement Impact ($)) ' for the selected period.

Reimbursement Impact for an encounter = (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) * Blended Rate.

Note:
Total Reimbursement Impact ($) rounded to 0 decimal places.

Format: $2,905,106
Overall Review Rate

Review Rate for the selected period.

Out of total encounters, the percentage of encounters reviewed.

Formula: Review Rate= Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) / Total encounters (Discharges) x 100.

Rounded to 0 decimal places. Example: 89%

Format: 96%

 Encounters with Review Not Needed encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Overall Clarification Rate

Encounter Clarification Rate for the selected period.

The percentage of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED.

Formula: Encounter Clarification Rate= (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED/ Count of Initial Reviews performed) *100

Format: 33%

 Encounters with Review Not Needed encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.

Provider Response Rate This metric displays the percentage of sent clarifications responded by the providers for each View By option.

Table 6. Reimbursement Impact Metrics
Metric Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria (if any) Tooltip
Reimbursement Impact

This metric displays the Reimbursement Impact for all the Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, Unassigned Impact) encounters with F-DRG WT > R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT > 0) and F-DRG WT < R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT < 0).

Reimbursement Impact ($) = The sum of Reimbursement Impact ($) (CMI Shift (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) x Blended Rate) for the Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, Unassigned Impact) encounters with F-DRG WT > R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT > 0) and F-DRG WT < R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT < 0)

Note:
Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, Unassigned Impact for Discharge Date < 11/14/2019) encounters without a shift in the WT (F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT = 0 or F-DRG = R-DRG WT) are not considered in the numerator.

Table 11. Footnotes
Formulas Text on hover
Review Rate

Total Encounters= Count of inpatient discharges with status other than 'Review Not Needed'.

Encounters Reviewed= Count of encounters reviewed with or without clarifications.

Review Rate= (Encounters Reviewed/ Total Encounters) x 100. E.g. 89%
Encounter Clarification Rate Encounter Clarification Rate= (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one clarification with the status other than REMOVED or RETRACTED/ Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews)) x100. E.g. 33%
Financial Impact Clarification Rate Financial Impact Clarification Rate = (The count of reviewed encounters with at least one Financial Impact Clarification with the status other than REMOVED or RETRACTED/Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) ) x 100). E.g. 30%
Provider Response Rate Provider Response Rate =(Count of clarifications with a Final response as Agreed, Not Agreed, Other Explanation, Alternative Specificity Diagnosis, Discuss, Sent to another Provider or Unable to Determine/Count of Clarifications Sent) x100. E.g. 90%
Provider Agreed Rate Provider Agreed Rate =(Count of clarifications with a Final response as Agreed/Count of Clarifications Responded) x100. E.g. 90%
CMI Before CDI The avg. of R-DRG WTs (or W-DRG WT if R-DRG is blank) of all Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Both, Financial Impact) encounters and F-DRG WTs of all Reconciled-No Impact, Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: SOI/ROM Impact) and Auto Reconciled encounters.
CMI After CDI The avg. of Final DRG WTs of all reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters.
CMI Shift

CMI Shift = (CMI After - CMI Before);

CMI Shift(%)= (CMI After - CMI Before)/CMI Before X 100. E.g. 3.14%
Reimbursement Impact Reimbursement Impact ($) = The sum of Reimbursement Impact ($) (CMI Shift (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) x Blended Rate) for the Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, Unassigned Impact) encounters with F-DRG WT > R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT > 0) and F-DRG WT < R-DRG WT (Or F-DRG WT – R-DRG WT < 0).
SOI/ROM Impact

% cases with SOI Impact= (Count of Reconciled encounters with SOI After > SOI Before/Count of Reconciled encounters.) X100

% cases with ROM Impact= (Count of Reconciled encounters with ROM After >ROM Before/Count of Reconciled encounters.) X100
