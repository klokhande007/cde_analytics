About CDE Analytics Reports

The following are few important points about reports:
  • Analytics reports are updated to display the Grouper, WT values, Reimbursement calculations as per the latest CMS 41.0 updates.
  • Data related to any cancelled admission is not displayed in reports after ADF refresh.
  • When you click "Reset" on the reports, they display data from the past three years. Older data is replaced by more recent data over time. By default, only the most recent data is shown.
