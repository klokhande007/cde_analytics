Team Performance

Overview: A CDS Team Performance dashboard helps a CDI Manager to analyze the overall performance of the individual CDSs and compare team performance.

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > Staff Management > Team Performance.

Team Performance displays the following:
  • KPI cards: Review Rate, Clarification Rate, Financial Clarification Rate, Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hrs)
  • Reviews chart
  • Clarification Rate chart
  • Provider Response and Agreed Rate chart
  • Case Mix Index chart
  • SOI/ROM Impact chart
  • CDS/Coder Match chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the tabular chart.

For filters all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the dashboard displays the data for the inpatient encounters discharged in the last three calendar months. Click the ‘Reset’ button to reset the discharge date and select the required date. When you reset the discharge date, the encounters that do not have a discharge date assigned are not displayed.

Review Rate for the selected time period.

Out of total encounters, the percentage of encounters reviewed.

Review Rate= Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) / Total encounters (Admissions/Discharges) x 100.

Out of total encounters reviewed, the percentage of sent clarifications with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED.

 Clarification Rate = (Count of clarifications with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED sent for the reviewed encounters/Count of Initial Reviews performed)*100 Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Format: 25%

 Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Financial Clarification Rate Financial Impact Clarification Rate for the selected time period.

Out of total encounters reviewed, the percentage of Encounters with Financial Impact Clarifications.

Clarification with a standard or custom ‘Primary Type at Reconciliation’ defined as ‘Financial or BOTH’ in the admin configuration is a Financial Impact Clarification.

 Financial Impact Clarification Rate = (Encounters with Financial Impact Clarifications /Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews) )* 100)Rounded to 0 decimal places.

Financial Impact Clarification Rate = (Count of reviewed encounters with at least one Financial Impact clarification with the status other than DELETED or RETRACTED / Count of encounters with an initial review date) X 100

Format: 37%

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hrs) Avg. Initial Review TAT (Hours) for the selected time period.

The number of hours between encounter Admit Date/time and the date/time the Working Review was first created.

(Initial Review TAT (hours) for an encounter = Initial Review Date/Time- Admit Date/Time) hours

Avg. Initial Review TAT(Hours) = Sum of Initial Review TAT(Hours) of all the reviewed encounters / Encounters Reviewed (Initial Reviews)

Format: 150

 Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.

  • Info icon(text on hover): CMI metrics are for all the reconciled cases. Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled.
Table 5. Case Mix Index chart Metrics
Metric Description Formula/Calculation Exclusion Criteria(if any) Tooltip
CMI Shift % This metric displays the percentage CMI shift for every month.

CMI Shift % = (The sum of CMI Shift (CMI After CDI - CMI Before CDI) for the Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, Unassigned Impact with shift in the WT) encounters that meet the report criteria) / (CMI Before CDI for Auto Reconciled, Reconciled-No Impact, Reconciled-Impact (Impact Type: Financial, Both, SOI/ROM Impact Unassigned Impact) encounters that meet the report criteria). X 100. (Rounded to 2 decimal places. E.g. 2.74%.)

1. CMI Before CDI: Average of Reconciliation Assessment DRG WTs (or Working DRG WT when Discharge Date < 11/14/19 or Reconciliation Assessment DRG is blank) of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

2. CMI After CDI: Average of Final DRG WTs of all encounters with status of Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

3. CMI Shift: Shift in weight between Final Coded DRG and Reconciliation Assessment DRG weights for Reconciled Encounters (Auto- Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact or Reconciled-No Impact) for which Reconciliation Assessment DRG WT (or W-DRG WT) is less than or equal to Final DRG WT (Final WT- R-DRG WT >= 0).

CDS/Coder Match Rate

The CDS/Coder Match Rate for each CDS.

CDS/Coder Match Rate = (Count of Reconciled Encounters with Match =Y / Count of Reconciled Encounters in the selected date range) *100

Tooltip 1:

CDS/Coder Match Rate: The total Count of Reconciled Encounters with Match =Y / Count of Reconciled Encounters in the selected date range *100

Matched Encounters: The total count of reconciled encounters where Final Coded DRG matches with Possible DRG (Working DRG if Possible DRG = Blank)

Reconciled Encounters: The total count of Reconciled encounters (Auto

Reconciled, Reconciled-Impact and Reconciled-No Impact)
CDS/Coder Match Logic
  • Compares Final Coded DRG to the Possible DRG (or Working DRG when Possible DRG is Blank)
  • Reconciliation Assessment DRG is NOT used to calculate the CDS/Coder Match.
  • For reconciled encounters without Possible DRG (i.e. Possible DRG is Blank), CDS/Coder Match is "Y" when Final Coded DRG matches with Working DRG.
  • For reconciled encounters with Possible DRG , CDS/Coder Match is "Y" when Final Coded DRG matches with Possible DRG.
Note:
Applicable to all Reconciled cases (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled) and Grouper Types (MS-DRG and APR Groupers)
