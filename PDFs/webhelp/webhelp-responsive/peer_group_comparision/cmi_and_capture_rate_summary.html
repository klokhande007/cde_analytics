CMI and Capture Rate Summary

Overview: The CMI and Capture Rate Summary dashboard provides insight into additional elements important to the management of the CDI program. The report includes month-to-month facility trends as well as comparisons to the hospital’s peer group. The data includes case mix index, secondary diagnosis capture, and medical/surgical proportions to assist the CDI manager and senior leadership in monitoring the health of their CDI program.

By default, the dashboard displays the data for reconciled inpatient encounters discharged in the last twelve calendar months.

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDE One > > Peer Group Comparison > CMI and Capture Rate Summary.

The CMI and Capture Rate Summary dashboard displays the following charts:
  • Case Mix Index
  • MCC Pair Capture
  • MCC Triplet Capture
  • CC Triplet Capture
  • CMI and Capture Rate Summary

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report Slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in the charts.

For filters on all pages, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Case Mix Index

This chart displays the average complexity and resource use of patient cases, reflecting clinical and financial performance.

  • X-axis: Displays the discharge month based on discharge date selection. (Format: Oct, Nov)
  • Y-axis:
    • CMI After CDI - The avg. of Final DRG WTs of all reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters.
    • PG CMI - The avg. of Final DRG WTs of all Peer Group reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters.

Case Mix Index Chart

MCC Pair Capture

This chart displays how often Major Complications or Comorbidities (MCC) are captured in pairs, compared with the peer group.

  • X-axis: Displays the discharge month based on discharge date selection. (Format: Oct, Nov).
  • Y-axis:
    • MCC Pair Capture Rate: Percent of reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W MCC” and DRG is part of a DRG pair.
    • PG MCC Pair Capture Rate: Percent of Peer Group reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W MCC” and DRG is part of a DRG pair.

Info Icon (Text on hover): Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled

MCC Pair Capture Chart

MCC Triplet Capture

This chart tracks the capture of MCC in triplets, helping measure coding accuracy and completeness against the peer group.

  • X-axis: Displays the discharge month based on discharge date selection. (Format: Oct, Nov)
  • Y-axis:
    • MCC Triplet Capture Rate: Percent of reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W MCC” and DRG is part of a DRG triplet.
    • PG MCC Triplet Capture Rate: Percent of Peer Group reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W MCC” and DRG is part of a DRG triplet.

Info Icon (Text on hover): Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled

CC Triplet Capture

This chart displays the rate at which Complications or Comorbidities (CC) are captured in triplets, compared to the peer group.

  • X-axis: Displays the discharge month based on discharge date selection. (Format: Oct, Nov)
  • Y-axis:
    • CC Triplet Capture Rate: Percent of reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W CC” and DRG is part of a DRG triplet.
    • PG CC Triplet Capture Rate: Percent of Peer Group reconciled (Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto Reconciled) encounters for Final DRG “W CC” and DRG is part of a DRG triplet.

Info Icon (Text on hover): Encounter Status: Reconciled-Impact, Reconciled-No Impact, Auto-Reconciled.

CMI and Capture Rate Summary

This chart displays your facility’s Case Mix Index (CMI) after CDI and the capture rates for MCC pairs, MCC triplets, and CC triplets. The dashboard also compares your metrics with peer group (PG) benchmarks across different months, ending with a total summary.
