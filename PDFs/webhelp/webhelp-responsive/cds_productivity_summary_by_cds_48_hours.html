Productivity Summary by CDS - 48 Hours

Overview: The report displays the data for the activities performed in the last 48 hours including today's date. For Example, when you open the report on 5/10/2021 at 4 PM EST, the report displays the activities performed in the last 48 hours – 5/8/2021 4PM EST – 5/10/2021 4 PM EST. This report displays activity data only for the CDS or CDS Manager.
Note:
A CDS can view only their data and not the data of their peers from same or different facility.
You need a real-time ‘Productivity Summary by CDS for the following:
  • A CDS Manager can get up-to-date information on individual CDS productivity to take necessary actions and follow-ups with CDS to improve overall CDI Program performance.
  • A CDS can view and analyze my productivity concurrently.
The report provides answers to the following business questions:
  • How is the overall performance of the CDS team in terms of review activity?
  • How is the performance of a specific CDS in terms of review activity?
  • How is the overall performance of the CDS team in terms of clarification activity?
  • How is the performance of a specific CDS in terms of clarification activity?
  • Who are my top three CDS performers in terms of the review rate

Navigation: On the Analytics home page left side panel, navigate to CDS General > CDS Productivity > Productivity Summary by CDS - 48 Hours.

The report displays the following:
  • Review Summary tabular chart
  • Clarification Summary tabular chart

On the top panel, you can select desired values for the slicers (refer Report slicers ) and the corresponding data gets filtered in charts.

For Global filters, refer Filters on all Pages.

For Footnotes, refer Footnotes.

Default Display: By default, the chart displays the data for the activity performed by the CDS in the last 48 hours (including today)

Table 1. Review Summary Table Column and Description
Metric Description
Initial Reviews

Displays the count of encounters with an initial review date/time in the selected activity date range.

NOTE:

Initial Review Date/Time falls in the selected activity date range.

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
Additional Reviews

Displays the count of additional reviews performed in the selected activity date range.

NOTE:

Additional Review Date/Time falls in the selected activity date range.

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.

Additional Review Types:
  • Clarification creation
  • Clarification modification
  • Working review modification
  • Worksheet creation or modification
  • Possible review creation
  • Possible review modification
  • Reconciliation assessment creation
  • Reconciliation assessment modification
  • Encounter Saved
  • Note creation or modification
  • Tracking Codes Addition or Modification
  • Encounter status modification

Activity Type 'Post-Discharge Review' is not considered in the Additional Reviews calculation.
Total Reviews This column displays the count of total reviews performed in the selected activity date range.

Total Reviews = (Initial Reviews + Additional Reviews)

NOTE:

Review Date/Time (Initial Review and Additional Review dates) falls in the selected activity date range.

Encounters with "Review Not Needed" encounter status are excluded from this metric calculations.
