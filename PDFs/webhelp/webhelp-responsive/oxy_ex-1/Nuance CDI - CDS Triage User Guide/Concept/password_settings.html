Password Settings

  • The maximum password age is 90 days.
  • Password once changed can only be updated after a minimum of one day.
  • While logging into the application, if you enter a wrong password five times, your account gets locked out, as you cross the maximum failed attempts allowed.
    Note:
    After the last failed login attempt, your account stays locked out for 15 minutes.
